## üìê Logo Design Guidelines

### Icon Requirements

**For iOS App Store**:
- 1024 x 1024 pixels
- PNG format
- No alpha channel (no transparency)
- No rounded corners (iOS applies automatically)
- Fill entire square, or use background color

**For Android Play Store**:
- Adaptive Icon:
  - Foreground: 432 x 432 pixels (centered in 1024x1024)
  - Background: 1024 x 1024 pixels (solid color or simple gradient)
- Legacy Icon: 512 x 512 pixels

### Design Principles

**DO**:
- ‚úÖ Keep it simple (recognizable at 44x44 pixels)
- ‚úÖ Use 2-3 colors maximum
- ‚úÖ Make it unique and memorable
- ‚úÖ Ensure it works in grayscale
- ‚úÖ Test at multiple sizes

**DON'T**:
- ‚ùå Use text in app icon (hard to read)
- ‚ùå Include thin lines (disappear when small)
- ‚ùå Copy competitors too closely
- ‚ùå Use photos or complex imagery
- ‚ùå Rely on fine details

## üéØ Success Criteria for Logo

A great logo should be:
- ‚úÖ **Memorable**: Recall after 10 seconds
- ‚úÖ **Scalable**: Looks good at 16px and 1024px
- ‚úÖ **Appropriate**: Fits fashion-tech niche
- ‚úÖ **Simple**: 3-year-old can draw it (roughly)
- ‚úÖ **Timeless**: Won't look dated in 5 years
- ‚úÖ **Unique**: Doesn't confuse with competitors

**Test**:
- Show to 10 people for 5 seconds
- Ask them to describe it from memory
- Ask "What do you think this app does?"
- 70%+ should guess correctly
